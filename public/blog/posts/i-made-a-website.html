I made a website!

February 19, 2026

I really didn't wanna do work (like 2 hours of coursework)... so I spent hours building a website instead? I was inspired by my friends Dexter and Josh who both have very cool websites by the way, check them out, bought a domain, and got designing!

This website uses React, Vite and Typescript. Before today, I mainly had experience with Python, Java and Haskell (literally only the programming languages I have to learn for University) So I suppose there was quite a steep learning curve trying to get the initial pages together.

As for the design, I really liked the idea of Soph's Space, it's my own little space on the internet! The icons and logos around the website are also space themed, but in the astrological sense of the word, hence, planet! I then threw it together in Canva, and here it is!

I don't expect my website to look like this or have this logo for forever, but it's really nice to see it just exist in the online world! ESPECIALLY after how long it took to get it up and running on the Cloudflare servers with basically no guide and pure willpower. I hope this will be the first of many, many blog posts, but first, I should really do my course work.
